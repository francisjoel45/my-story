Chapter One

The Cafeteria Table

It was one of those normal school days—nothing special about the timetable, nothing planned. I was sitting in the cafeteria with his friends and mine too, passing time the usual way: jokes, laughter, and a deck of cards spread across the table.

That day, one of his friends brought someone along. She wasn't new to them—but she was new to me. Her name was Zom-bea.

"She had been my long-time crush, someone i admired quietly from a distance. I had never found the courage to approach her before."

That day felt different. For the first time, she was right there—laughing, talking, close enough to feel real. I knew i might not get another moment like this. So instead of staying silent, I took the chance.
